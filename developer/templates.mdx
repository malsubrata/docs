TeraWallet includes template files that can be overridden in your theme.

## How to Override
1. Copy the template from `wp-content/plugins/woo-wallet/templates/`.  
2. Paste it into your theme under: yourtheme/woo-wallet/
3. Modify safely — your changes will override plugin defaults.

## Example Templates
- `woo-wallet/wc-endpoint-wallet.php` → Displays wallet transactions  
- `woo-wallet/wc-wallet-topup-form.php` → Form for adding funds  
- `woo-wallet/wc-transfer-form.php` → Form for wallet transfers  

⚠️ **Tip**: Always check for updated templates when upgrading the plugin.
